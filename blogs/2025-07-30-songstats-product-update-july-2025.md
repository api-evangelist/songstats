<h3>Songstats: Product Update — July 2025</h3><h4>Venue Recommendations, Single Sign-On, Radiostats Improvements &amp; more…</h4><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*5hjE2zFc7T3ca4UqHb07Bg.jpeg" /></figure><h3><strong>Venue Recommendations</strong></h3><p>The <a href="https://songstats.com/app">Songstats</a> legacy tool “Venue Finder” has been deprecated and is now replaced with our shiny new <strong>Venue Recommendations</strong>! This upgrade brings our state-of-the-art recommendation algorithm to event data and surfaces the best venues for you to perform at.</p><p>Songstats provides detailed information for each venue, previous and upcoming events, as well as social links for you to reach out to and plan your next tour. Venue Recommendations are now available via the Marketing Tools in both the Songstats desktop dashboard and the latest version of the mobile app.</p><h3>Sign In Through Google, Facebook &amp; Apple</h3><p>We’ve further enhanced our Sign-In experience and are now supporting <strong>Single Sign-On with Google, Facebook and Apple</strong> via the desktop dashboard and mobile app. For existing members, you can connect one or more third-party accounts directly via Account Settings to enable them for SSO and speed up your login experience.</p><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*FMVH17kTl5HKjMqucaVVEQ.jpeg" /></figure><h3>Radiostats Improvements</h3><p>Over the past few months we’ve continued our efforts to improve <a href="https://radiostats.com">Radiostats</a> and have added over <strong>5,000+ new stations</strong>, increasing our total number of stations that are actively being monitored to over 50,000. We also advanced the fingerprinting of all songs in our database to ensure more accurate results.</p><h3>Additional Improvements &amp; Bug Fixes</h3><ul><li>Added New Job Server</li><li>Switched to IndexedDB</li><li>Upgraded Backend Framework</li><li>Improved State Management</li></ul><p>Version 8.0.0 of the <a href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/songstats/id1436000800">iOS</a> and <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.trackstats">Android App</a> is now available and recommended to be installed by all users to take advantage of the new features and fixes.</p><img alt="" height="1" src="https://medium.com/_/stat?event=post.clientViewed&amp;referrerSource=full_rss&amp;postId=31ae20cf0825" width="1" /><hr /><p><a href="https://lab.songstats.com/songstats-product-update-july-2025-31ae20cf0825">Songstats: Product Update — July 2025</a> was originally published in <a href="https://lab.songstats.com">The Songstats Lab</a> on Medium, where people are continuing the conversation by highlighting and responding to this story.</p>
