<h3>Songstats: Product Update — August 2025</h3><h4>Artist Recommendations, Related Labels, Improved Catalog Imports &amp; more…</h4><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*JURNDVVDesRZ70UxlXM9Ig.jpeg" /></figure><h3>Artist Recommendations</h3><p><a href="https://songstats.com">Songstats</a> now offers a new Marketing Tool for all label customers: <strong>Artist Recommendations</strong>. This feature mirrors our popular Label Recommendations (available for artist profiles) and helps label owners and A&amp;Rs discover emerging talents that match their sound.</p><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*GMEZp6IpPv_iwTPlI1iQ5A.jpeg" /></figure><h3>Related Labels</h3><p><a href="https://songstats.com/app">Songstats</a> now displays Related Labels on all Artist Overview Pages and presents new Artist &amp; Label Popups to give a quick snapshot of recent performance metrics of releases from the past 6 months — including average streams, peak popularity, video creations, Shazam tags and DJ supports.</p><h3>Improved Catalog Imports</h3><p>We significantly improved the automated aggregation of collaborator catalogs, providing a more comprehensive view of all works a collaborator has contributed to. The automatic import of historic and new releases into label catalogs has also been improved to further enhance usability of the platform. We invite all collaborators to check and sync their catalogs, to showcase and monitor their works across the industry.</p><h3>Additional Improvements &amp; Bug Fixes</h3><ul><li>Refined Artist Recommendations</li><li>Improved SiriusXM Monitoring</li></ul><p>Version 8.1.0 of the <a href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/songstats/id1436000800">iOS</a> and <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.trackstats">Android App</a> is now available and recommended to be installed by all users to take advantage of the new features and fixes.</p><img alt="" height="1" src="https://medium.com/_/stat?event=post.clientViewed&amp;referrerSource=full_rss&amp;postId=7706a6c89582" width="1" /><hr /><p><a href="https://lab.songstats.com/songstats-product-update-august-2025-7706a6c89582">Songstats: Product Update — August 2025</a> was originally published in <a href="https://lab.songstats.com">The Songstats Lab</a> on Medium, where people are continuing the conversation by highlighting and responding to this story.</p>
