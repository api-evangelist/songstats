<figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/0*-0USHiQQse0jiWtz" /></figure><p><a href="https://wearecrossfader.co.uk/"><strong>Crossfader</strong></a> is a leading DJ education platform designed to help aspiring and established DJs improve their skills across all major software and hardware. Beyond structured courses and tutorials, Crossfader has built a thriving global community where DJs can connect, collaborate, and learn from one another. Their presence extends across YouTube, Instagram, and podcasts, offering free tips, insights, and inspiration to DJs at every stage of their journey.</p><p>We’ve teamed up with Crossfader — a natural fit given Songstats’ heritage in the electronic music world — and we’re bridging the gap between data-driven insights and hands-on DJ education. As part of the collaboration, all Songstats subscribers can benefit from <strong>20% off Crossfader’s courses</strong>. This exclusive discount code is available directly in the <strong>Benefits</strong> area of your Songstats dashboard.</p><p>We caught up with <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/richard-lawrence-bb98368b/">Lawrence James</a>, Marketing Manager at Crossfader, to chat about the platform, the world of DJing, and his top tips for career growth! Read the full interview below.</p><a href="https://medium.com/media/00b7583fca502c5d77e56d39e589e249/href">https://medium.com/media/00b7583fca502c5d77e56d39e589e249/href</a><p><strong>Q: What is Crossfader, what inspired its creation, and who is it for?</strong></p><p><strong>Lawrence: </strong>Crossfader is a global learning platform designed to teach the world how to DJ. At its heart, we’re a team of passionate DJs who have experienced the highs and lows of the industry, playing residencies, travelling internationally, and performing to both packed clubs and empty rooms. Those early experiences showed us what was missing in traditional DJ education and inspired us to create something better.</p><p>We created Crossfader to remove the common barriers that stop people from getting started. First, we focus on inspiring people through engaging DJ mix content, insightful podcast interviews with professional DJs, and accessible free tutorials. Then we started guiding learners with structured, easy-to-follow online courses, taken by students in more than 100 countries every year. Finally, we support our students and fans through a vibrant online community of over 1.5 million DJs. Whether someone is mixing in their bedroom or preparing for their first gig, Crossfader is here to help at every stage.</p><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/0*pELV5TnZinuZ9t68" /></figure><p><strong>Q: How does Crossfader teach DJing differently from other platforms or traditional music schools, and what makes your methodology effective?</strong></p><p><strong>Lawrence: </strong>What makes Crossfader different is our personalised approach to learning. While platforms like YouTube or celebrity-led masterclasses can offer general advice, they rarely address the specific needs of each individual. At Crossfader, we teach students based on their own setup, the genres they love, the skill level they are at, and the goals they want to reach.</p><p>When a student signs up, we ask them a few simple questions. Their answers help us recommend the right courses and tutorials for their unique journey. This tailored learning path makes their experience more effective and enjoyable.</p><p>We offer a wide range of gear-specific courses for controllers such as the Pioneer DJ DDJ-FLX4, XDJ series, and CDJs. We also cover popular DJ software like Serato DJ Pro, Traktor, rekordbox, and more. In addition, we provide genre-focused courses for House, Techno, Drum &amp; Bass, Hip-Hop, and Open Format DJs.</p><p>Our strong partnerships with leading equipment and software companies allow us to stay current with every new release and feature. Many of these partners have officially certified our content, giving our students confidence that they are learning to a professional standard. As soon as updates or innovations are released, we are ready to teach them. This keeps our students up to date and ahead of the curve.</p><p><strong>Q: In what ways do you think DJs could best leverage data analytics from Songstats to grow their careers?</strong></p><p><strong>Lawrence: </strong>Songstats is an essential tool for DJs who want to grow their careers in a smart and strategic way. It collects real-time data from major platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, TikTok, SoundCloud, Beatport, 1001Tracklists, and Shazam. All of this information is displayed clearly in one dashboard, making it easy to track music performance, audience growth, and industry trends.</p><p>For beginner DJs, Songstats can highlight early signs of success. For example, if a new track gets picked up by a playlist or gains streams in a specific country, the artist can create content targeted at that region, run ads, or pitch to local blogs and promoters. These small wins can quickly turn into bigger opportunities, and Songstats helps you spot them early.</p><p>For more established DJs, the platform becomes a professional planning tool. If you notice your track is charting on Beatport or being played by DJs in a specific scene, you can use that data to tailor your live sets, approach labels, or plan tours. You can also showcase achievements in your press kit or on social media to attract collaborations and brand partnerships.</p><p>The real power of Songstats is that it turns data into action. It helps DJs understand what’s working and why, so they can grow their audience, market their music effectively, and make confident decisions about their career.</p><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/0*xuf8jMgiQJjH9auB" /></figure><p><strong>Q: Support and collaboration amongst DJs is extremely important in building a reputation and creating opportunities. How do you foster a sense of community for DJs who learn online?</strong></p><p><strong>Lawrence: </strong>Crossfader is more than just a place to learn DJing. It’s a supportive community where DJs can grow, connect, and celebrate each other’s progress. We take pride in offering personal support to every student, whether they need technical help, creative advice, or encouragement.</p><p>Every question inside our courses gets answered by a real DJ. Emails are personally handled by Liv, our customer experience manager, who ensures that no one is left waiting. Our social media manager, Eve, is constantly on the lookout for emerging talent. She showcases DJs across our platforms and even attends events to meet creators in person.</p><p>Our Discord community has grown to over 5,000 DJs from around the world. It’s an active space where members share mixes, ask questions, give feedback, and even start collaborations. It’s like being part of a worldwide DJ collective.</p><p>We also show up in person. We’ve hosted workshops at Apple Music, run education tours at Pirate Studios, supported female-led events like Women in Drum &amp; Bass, and even surprised one of our students at her birthday party to watch her perform. We genuinely care about our community, and our actions reflect that.</p><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/0*UrMPxfqVDYna3Cmx" /></figure><p><strong>Q: Lastly, where do you see the future of DJing heading, and what role will Crossfader play in shaping that future?</strong></p><p><strong>Lawrence: </strong>The future of DJing is full of opportunity. DJs are now some of the most influential performers in the world, and there are more paths than ever to becoming one. Whether someone wants to play club residencies, host radio shows, perform at weddings, build a brand online, or headline major festivals, the options are endless.</p><p>At Crossfader, our mission is to guide each DJ along their own path. We are committed to providing personalised education and support so every learner gets exactly what they need to succeed. Whether you’re playing your first mix or preparing a festival set, we’ll be there with the tools, guidance, and motivation you need.</p><p>We also recognise that many DJs want to evolve into full artists by producing their own music. That’s why we’re now investing in music production education. This will allow us to offer a complete journey, from learning to mix, to performing live, to creating original music.</p><p>Crossfader is not just keeping up with the future of DJing. We are helping shape it. Our goal is to inspire the next generation, support them through every step, and give them the confidence to turn their passion into a lifelong career.</p><img alt="" height="1" src="https://medium.com/_/stat?event=post.clientViewed&amp;referrerSource=full_rss&amp;postId=c1c9bd078814" width="1" /><hr /><p><a href="https://lab.songstats.com/songstats-crossfader-unite-for-the-next-generation-of-djs-c1c9bd078814">Songstats &amp; Crossfader Unite For The Next Generation Of DJs</a> was originally published in <a href="https://lab.songstats.com">The Songstats Lab</a> on Medium, where people are continuing the conversation by highlighting and responding to this story.</p>
