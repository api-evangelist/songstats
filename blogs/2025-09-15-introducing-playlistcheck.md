<h4>The Ultimate Tool for Spotify Playlist Insights and Health Validation</h4><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*MUdAm8_ITIaFqS2hVfvQcw.jpeg" /></figure><p>Today, we’re thrilled to announce the launch of <a href="https://playlistcheck.com/"><strong>Playlistcheck</strong></a>, the ultimate playlist analytics platform that is providing detailed performance insights and health validations in real-time.</p><p>Whether you’re an artist pitching your latest track, a label scouting for placement opportunities, or a curator fine-tuning your selections, <a href="https://playlistcheck.com">Playlistcheck</a> empowers you with the data you need to optimize your playlist strategies.</p><p>Powered by <a href="https://songstats.com/">Songstats</a>, Playlistcheck transforms raw playlist data into actionable insights. Let’s explore what makes this tool a game-changer.</p><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*mDunDJmNh3Pvy4A_vI0MxA.png" /></figure><h3><strong>Uncover Playlist Performance at a Glance</strong></h3><p>In the ever-evolving world of music streaming, playlists are the lifeblood of discovery for artists, labels, and fans alike. Playlistcheck offers a transparent look into the <strong>metadata, growth, and curation patterns </strong>of any playlist on Spotify, along with curator contacts and a clear assessment of whether a playlist is worth pitching to.</p><p>Here’s a look at six key metrics that Playlistcheck is unveiling.</p><h4><strong>1. Augmented Metadata</strong></h4><p>Playlistcheck aggregates advanced metadata like:</p><ul><li><strong>Playlist Type</strong> (Editorial / Algorithmic / Listener)</li><li><strong>Last Update </strong>(1 day ago)</li><li><strong>Update Frequency</strong> (weekly)</li><li><strong>Playlist Length</strong> (100 songs)</li><li><strong>Average Placement Duration</strong> (97 days)</li></ul><p>This information helps you to quickly determine how actively a playlist is being curated and how long you can expect your song to be part of the selection once it has been added.</p><h4><strong>2. Audio Insights</strong></h4><p>Get an in-depth audio analysis of the songs on the playlist, including <strong>average track popularity, release date, and audio features</strong> covering danceability, loudness, acousticness, and energy. These metrics are great to see if a playlist is predominantly featuring older hits, or if the curator helps to surface newer, emerging releases.</p><h4><strong>3. Follower Growth Tracking</strong></h4><p>Visualize follower trends over time with intuitive charts to check if a playlist has been growing in popularity or if it has been artificially inflated. The follower growth over the past 28 days provides much needed context over just the total number of saves, as it can clearly show if a playlist recently has been trending or if it is no longer relevant.</p><h4><strong>4. Playlist Health Scores</strong></h4><p>Playlistcheck uses a proprietary algorithm to calculate an overarching health score for each playlist. This score gives you a clear indication if a playlist is frequently being refreshed, how influential it is, and how likely and beneficial it would be for a new release to be featured.</p><h4><strong>5. Global &amp; Genre Ranks</strong></h4><p>Playlistcheck is the only place that provides accurate standings of playlists across the entire Spotify ecosystem. The platform ranks each playlist against all global playlists, as well as the biggest playlists in its genre. This insight is invaluable for you to identify the top playlists for your sound.</p><h4><strong>6. Featured Artists, Labels &amp; Songs</strong></h4><p>Get a comprehensive overview of current placements on the playlist and listen to audio previews of the top songs to cross-check that your music would be the right fit. Playlistcheck also recommends similar playlists for you to keep exploring.</p><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*o8XcI4Dl8GJlDgAMM9R9BA.png" /></figure><h3>Ready to pitch your music?</h3><p><a href="https://playlistcheck.com">Playlistcheck</a> provides one-click opportunities to contact curators seamlessly through partner integrations with <strong>SubmitHub</strong>, <strong>Groover</strong>, <strong>Matchfy</strong>, <strong>Daily Playlists</strong>, and <strong>SongRocket</strong>. Available contact options also include social media channels and email addresses for you to submit your new release in a breeze.</p><h3><strong>Why Playlistcheck Matters for the Music Industry</strong></h3><p>In a landscape where playlist placements can make or break a release, <a href="https://playlistcheck.com">Playlistcheck</a> levels the playing field. Artists and labels can validate playlist quality before pitching, identify high-potential curators, and track performance over time. Curators benefit from health checks that ensure authenticity and growth. It’s not just data — it’s the edge you need to thrive on Spotify.</p><p><em>Ready to check the performance of any Spotify playlist?
