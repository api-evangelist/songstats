<h4><strong>Cross-Platform Music Insights for Artists, Labels &amp; Songs — right inside Chrome &amp; Firefox</strong></h4><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*PoJSOLMxZ-SnnCOZCbIcDQ.jpeg" /></figure><p>The new <strong>Songstats Browser Extension</strong> for <a href="https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/songstats-music-analytics/nmmbeehfomfgganaddfkilffejhdbakk"><strong>Google Chrome</strong></a> and <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/songstats-music-analytics/"><strong>Firefox</strong></a> brings powerful music analytics directly into your browsing experience. Instantly unlock cross-platform insights on any artist, label, or track — available right from the side panel as you explore streaming services, download stores, and social platforms.</p><p>Once installed, simply pin the Songstats icon to your toolbar. As you navigate, the icon will automatically highlight whenever Songstats data is available. Open an artist page on Spotify, watch a music video on YouTube, or view a label catalog on Beatport — the extension activates immediately and reveals deeper, aggregated insights on the spot.</p><figure><img alt="" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*zQdF0ST-suesw-yEYXcQVQ.png" /><figcaption>Songstats Browser Extension Side Panel on the right side</figcaption></figure><p>The side panel gives you fast access to metrics such as:</p><ul><li>Total Streams &amp; Radio Plays</li><li>Follower &amp; Subscriber Growth</li><li>Popularity Index &amp; Monthly Listeners</li><li>Advanced Metadata (ISRC, Distributor, Biography, etc)</li><li>Playlist Reach &amp; Chart Entries</li><li>Related Artists &amp; Labels</li><li>Audio Analysis</li></ul><p>… and much more.</p><p>The extension even includes <a href="https://playlistcheck.com"><strong>Playlistcheck</strong></a>, giving you in-depth playlist analytics as soon as you open a Spotify playlist — including playlist health, global and genre ranks, follower growth, and contact information.</p><p>No more tab-hopping, searching, or pulling up dashboards. Your music intelligence now follows you wherever you browse — instantly and effortlessly. And best of all, the extension is <strong>completely free</strong>.</p><p>Install the Songstats Browser Extension today (<a href="https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/songstats-music-analytics/nmmbeehfomfgganaddfkilffejhdbakk">Chromium</a> / <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/songstats-music-analytics/">Firefox</a>) and unlock the full story behind your favorite music!</p><img alt="" height="1" src="https://medium.com/_/stat?event=post.clientViewed&amp;referrerSource=full_rss&amp;postId=4c638c73d25e" width="1" /><hr /><p><a href="https://lab.songstats.com/new-songstats-feature-browser-extension-4c638c73d25e">New Songstats Feature: Browser Extension</a> was originally published in <a href="https://lab.songstats.com">The Songstats Lab</a> on Medium, where people are continuing the conversation by highlighting and responding to this story.</p>
