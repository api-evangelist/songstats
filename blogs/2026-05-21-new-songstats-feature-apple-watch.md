Your most important insights right at the flick of your wrist With the latest update to the Songstats iOS App we are thrilled to bring an awesome new feature directly to your Apple Watch! You can now track all of your most important insights like your Spotify Monthly Listeners, Instagram Followers, Apple Music Playlists, YouTube Subscribers, Total Shazams, Radio Plays, and more — without even unlocking your phone. The new Songstats watchOS experience is designed to keep your key metrics beautifully accessible throughout the day.
