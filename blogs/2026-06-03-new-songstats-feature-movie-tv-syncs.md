Songstats has launched a Movie & TV Syncs feature enabling artists and labels to track music placements across films and television shows, providing detailed information including movie titles, show titles, season and episode details, and featured timestamps. The tool helps creators discover how their catalog reaches audiences through screen media and is available in version 8.6.0 of the iOS and Android apps.
