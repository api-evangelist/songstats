Social Media Growth Insights for Creators & Brands Over the years, we’ve helped thousands of artists, labels, managers, and industry professionals understand what’s happening around their music. With Songstats , we brought together streaming, playlist, and radio data to help artists make better decisions. Along the way, one thing became increasingly clear: while releases are important, what happens between each release cycle is just as critical.
